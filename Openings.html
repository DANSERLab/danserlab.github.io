Openings


Overview: We are seeking applicants engineers or scientists (at Postdoctoral Scholar and PhD student levels) to join our multidisciplinary team developing hardware, software and cloud computing techniques that monitor physiological development of in-vivo, ex-vivo and in-vitro biological systems. Prior work with biological systems and biological integration is desirable, but optional. Duties will include designing and performing experiments and/or theoretical models, writing papers and reports, and working alongside undergraduate and graduate students.

  • Embedded Electronics: Applicants with research expertise in developing low noise electronic circuits (design and fabrication) and signal processing or similar fields are strongly encouraged to apply.

  • Brightfield and Fluorescence Imaging of Cellular Development: Applicants with research expertise in one or more of the following areas: Optics, Microimaging, Biological imaging, CMOS-based electronics, or similar fields are strongly encouraged to apply.

  • Microfluidics and Microfabrication: Applicants with research expertise in cleanroom microfabrication as well as developing microfluidics devices or similar fields are strongly encouraged to apply.

  • Neurodynamics - Applied Mathematics, Data Scientist, Maths, Time Simulations and Machine Learning: Applicants with research expertise in cleanroom microfabrication as well as developing microfluidics devices or similar fields are strongly encouraged to apply.

Please apply to the advertised positions or contact me if you have any questiuon or if you are interested in applying (mteodore@ucsc.edu). Please include "Openings" of in the e-mail subject.
